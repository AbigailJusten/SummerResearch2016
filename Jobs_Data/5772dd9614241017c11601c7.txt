Job Title: Healthcare

Information: Social workers in healthcare facilitate medical and emotional treatment of patients. They may focus on helping clients and their families cope with acute or terminal illnesses by providing psychosocial support.Choose volunteer settings such as hospitals, the American Red Cross, or AIDS agencies to get experience.Be prepared to work with multidisciplinary teams including doctors, nurses, health, and other mental health providers.Attend local support groups to begin to understand the impact of illness on the person, family, and other support systems.
