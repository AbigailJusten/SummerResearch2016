About Career Topics

Career Topics focuses on finding what skills, topics and areas of study are the most valuable for a variety of jobs. In order to find this information, a total of 3,654 job listings were collected and then run through a topic analysis.

Under Jobs you will find a listing of each job description that was gathered during this process. Within each job's description you will find the original data collected for the job followed by the topics that related most to the job. By selecting a topic you will find all of the jobs related to that topic.

Analysis Details

  • Number of Topics: 100
  • Number of Job Listings: 3654
  • Sources: What Can I Do With This Major (622 entries) and My Plan (3032 entries)
